What is the 4l trophy tour?

The Raid 4L Trophy is the first solidarity rally raid 100% student, young active and fun taking place in 4L. More than just a trip, it's a crazy experience, a journey of 6000 kilometers crossing 3 countries. The goal is to raise funds and bring school supplies to Morocco.

The Trek:

1: Departure to France from Biarritz

2: Crossing from Spain

3: Trek on the tracks Morocco
